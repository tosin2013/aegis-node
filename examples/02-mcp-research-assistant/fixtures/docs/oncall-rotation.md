# Oncall Rotation

Every senior engineer takes a one-week oncall rotation roughly every
six weeks. The rotation covers the full stack — there's no separate
SRE oncall.

Oncalls are paid for off-hours pages but not for being on the rotation
itself. The expectation is that oncall is part of senior engineering,
not a separate job function.

Severity levels: SEV1 (production down, paged immediately), SEV2
(degraded service, paged within 30 min), SEV3 (next business day).
